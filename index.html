♩ Music Toolkit

Small, focused tools for practicing and understanding music. Everything runs in the browser — no accounts, no installs, sound included.

Circle of Fifths

An interactive wheel of all twelve keys. Turn it to see each key's signature, scale, and the seven chords that belong to it — and hear them.

Chord Explorer

Pick a root and a quality — from plain triads to ninths — and see the chord spelled out on a piano keyboard, with intervals, and play it.

Scale Explorer

Browse scales and modes — major, the three minors, all seven modes, pentatonic, blues and more — spelled out, on the keyboard, played up or down.

Metronome

A steady click with accented downbeats, any time signature, tap tempo, and classical tempo names from Largo to Prestissimo.

Interval Ear Trainer

Hear two notes, name the interval. Ascending, descending, or harmonic — with a running score to track your progress.

A pitch pipe for tuning by ear: any note in any octave, sustained as a drone, with adjustable concert pitch (415–445 Hz).
